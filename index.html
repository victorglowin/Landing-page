Earthify

Climate Change is the defining issue of our time and we are at a defining moment. From shifting weather patterns that threaten food production, to rising sea levels that increase the risk of catastrophic flooding, the impacts of climate change are global in scope and unprecedented in scale. Earthify was founded in 2022 to contribute to the fight for a better planet through increased awareness and coordinated actions to tackle climate change.
